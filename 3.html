Cargo pants or cargo trousers, also sometimes called combat pants or combat trousers after their original purpose as military workwear,[1] are loosely cut pants originally designed for rough work environments and outdoor activities, distinguished by numerous large utility pockets for carrying tools. Cargo shorts are a shorts version of the cargo pants, with the legs usually extending down to near-knee lengths. Both cargo pants and shorts have since become also popular as an urban casual wear, since they are loose-fitting and quite convenient for carrying extra items during everyday foot trips or while cycling.
